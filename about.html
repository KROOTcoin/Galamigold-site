Who We Are

Gold at its
Source

Galami Gold is a Dubai-registered gold supply and trade finance company specialising in the procurement, import, and structured trading of physical gold sourced directly from Africa.

Founded on the principle that investors deserve direct access to commodity-backed returns — without the complexity of ETFs, derivatives, or speculative instruments — Galami operates at the intersection of African gold supply and Dubai's world-leading bullion market.

Our model is straightforward: we source gold from proven African supply chains, import and refine it in Dubai, trade it on the spot market, and return structured, trade-linked profits to our investors under transparent Loan Note Agreements.

Our Structure

A Multi-Entity
Group

Galami UK Ltd — London

Roycroft Precious Metals Trading FZCO

Dubai Silicon Oasis based import vehicle under the same ownership as Galami FZCO. Handles bullion import, refining coordination and logistics for the Guinea program.

Our Values

What Sets Us Apart

I.

Direct Sourcing

We work directly with miners and established refineries in Africa — cutting out layers of intermediaries and ensuring our investors benefit from source-level economics.

II.

Transparency First

Independent audits every 90 days, full trade reporting, and clear contractual terms under Loan Note Agreements. We believe investors deserve to see exactly where their capital is deployed.

III.

Trade-Linked Returns

Our investors earn from actual commodity trade margins — not speculative price movements. This creates more predictable, consistent returns tied to real physical transactions.

IV.

Ethical Supply Chain

All gold sourced under our programs is subject to conflict-free verification and chain-of-custody documentation aligned with OECD due diligence guidelines.

V.

Dubai Advantage

Dubai is the world's leading gold trading hub — offering deep liquidity, competitive spot pricing, regulatory clarity and proximity to African supply chains.

VI.

Investor Flexibility

No forced reinvestment. Exit at the end of any active trade cycle. Capital is not locked beyond operational requirements, giving investors genuine liquidity optionality.
