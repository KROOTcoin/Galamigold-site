Veracash

At a Glance

Veracash blends physical and digital savings so you hold insured gold and silver while managing everything from an app. It pairs Swiss storage with a Mastercard that allows spending metals in everyday transactions.

Core Features

Veracash offers physical storage of gold silver and diamonds with digital account management that displays holdings in grams. The service includes insured Swiss vaults, no minimum purchase amounts and mobile app access for buying selling and tracking metals.

Pros

  • Secure insured storage: Veracash stores physical metals in Swiss vaults with insurance protecting against loss and theft which suits conservative allocations.
  • Transparent pricing: The platform advertises no hidden fees and clear purchase and resale fee structures which aids portfolio planning.
  • Mobile account management: The mobile app makes it simple to view holdings in grams buy or sell metals and monitor valuations on the move.
  • Mastercard for payments: A Mastercard backed by your metal balance provides frictionless payment and withdrawal options for daily use.
  • Wide metal selection: You can purchase gold silver and diamonds offering diversification within precious metals rather than a single asset exposure.

Cons

  • Fees apply to some transactions such as credit card top ups and metal purchases which raises the effective cost of frequent activity.
  • Account access depends on digital infrastructure which creates operational risk if app or connectivity issues occur during critical moments.
  • Service availability is limited to certain countries for account opening which restricts access for internationally diversified investors.

Who It’s For

Veracash targets investors seeking tangible savings with digital convenience who want both custody and spendability. This includes high net worth individuals preparing inheritance plans savers diversifying away from bank deposits and investors seeking a portable store of value.

Unique Value Proposition

Veracash uniquely combines allocated physical custody with a payment card so your precious metal holdings are both secured in vaults and usable for everyday purchases. That duality appeals to investors who want discretionary liquidity without liquidating positions.

Real World Use Case

A user opens a Veracash account purchases grams of gold and silver manages positions via the mobile app and uses the Mastercard for routine expenses. The user retains physical ownership while reducing friction between savings and spending.

Pricing

Purchase service fees are around 3 percent of the transaction amount while bank transfer top ups are free and credit card top ups incur a 0.8 percent fee. Resale fees vary by method with some sell actions being fee free which influences exit timing.

Website: https://www.veracash.com
